KISHAN - A Virtual Farmer Assistant

About KISHAN

KISHAN is your AI-powered farming assistant, designed to guide farmers with crop information, pest diagnosis, and direct expert connections. With real-time data and smart insights, KISHAN helps you improve yields and reduce risks.

Why use KISHAN?

KISHAN combines modern AI-powered support, expert human interactions, and practical knowledge to ensure farming is more accessible, sustainable, and profitable. The assistant offers features such as real-time weather updates, pest and disease prediction, access to a knowledge database, and latest farming practices, making it a one-stop solution for all farming queries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What can KISHAN do?

A: Diagnose plant diseases, provide crop advice, connect you with experts, and more.

Q: Difference between chatbot and expert?

A: Chatbot gives instant info 24/7, while experts provide personalized, detailed advice.

Q: Is this free?

A: Yes, basic features are free. Advanced expert help may have a small cost.

Q: Can I upload images?

A: Yes, upload crop images in chat and KISHAN will analyze and guide you.
